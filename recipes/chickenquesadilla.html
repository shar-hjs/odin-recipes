Chicken Quesadilla

Ingredients

  • chicken (any part)
  • seasoning of choice
  • vegetables of choice (tomatoes, onions, peppers)
  • cheese
  • tortilla
  • sour cream (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat a pan or skillet with oil then add the chicken and seasoning. Add the vegetables and once cooked through, remove from heat.
  2. Place a tortilla on another heated pan. Layer half the tortilla with the chicken filling and cheese and fold the other half of the tortilla over. Cook until golden brown on both sides.
  3. Optional: Serve with a dollop of sour cream on the side or on the quesadilla.
